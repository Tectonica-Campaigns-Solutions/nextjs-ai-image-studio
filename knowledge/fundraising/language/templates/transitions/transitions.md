# Transition Language Templates

## Yes Path — Into Condensed Diagnostic

> "Great — a few quick questions before we build your plan."

## No Path — Into Full Diagnostic

> "Let's figure out the right approach for you. I'll ask a few questions about your group, your audience, and what you're working with — it won't take long and it'll make sure we recommend the right tactic."

## Sanity Check — Into Redirect

When redirecting from a flagged tactic to the full diagnostic:

> "Based on what you've told me, [tactic] isn't going to work well here — [one-sentence reason]. But what you're trying to do is absolutely doable. Let me ask a few more questions so I can point you to something that'll actually work."

## Into Tactic Recommendation

> "Based on everything you've told me, here's what I'd recommend:"

## Into Brief Generation

> "Let's put your plan together."

## Graphic Helper Handoff — Contextual (Within Execution Steps)

At the step in the execution plan where imagery is most useful:

> "This is a great moment to create your campaign visuals. Connect to the Graphic Helper → [link/button]"

## Graphic Helper Handoff — End of Brief

> "Your campaign plan is ready. Would you like to create imagery to go with it? Connect to the Graphic Helper →"
