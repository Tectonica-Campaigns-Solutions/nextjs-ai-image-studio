## When to retrieve from this folder

Retrieve **voice-reference.md** before generating any of the following for an All Out leader:
- Email appeals or fundraising emails
- Social media posts
- CTA language or ask scripts
- Thank-you messages
- Any copy where tone and voice matter

Retrieve **style-guide.md** before generating copy that involves:
- Identity or community language (gender, orientation, specific communities)
- Numbers, currency amounts, or dates
- US vs. UK dialect decisions
- Formatting for a specific channel (email, social, press)

Retrieve **both** when generating a full Campaign Brief for an All Out leader — voice and style rules apply throughout the CTA and Key Messaging section and all inline deliverables.
