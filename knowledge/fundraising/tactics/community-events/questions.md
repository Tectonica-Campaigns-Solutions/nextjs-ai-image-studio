# Community Events — Questions

Group only. Cannot be run by one person. Cash handling must be in place before proceeding.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have enough volunteers to plan and run the event? This tactic is very difficult for one person to manage alone."

> "For each person helping — what role are they best suited for? For example: event coordinator, cash handler, greeter, social media promoter, donor follow-up. We'll use this to assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "Do you have a venue or location confirmed, or a strong lead on one?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations at the event? If so, does your org have a process in place for handling and tracking cash?"

> "Have you checked whether you need any permits or permissions for this event — from your local council, venue, or relevant authority?"

> "Do you have materials to display or hand out at the event — signage, flyers, QR codes to donate?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have a story or cause connection we can feature at the event to make the fundraising ask more compelling?"

> "Do you have contact details for people you want to invite or promote to?"

> "Are you active on social media? We can help you promote the event."

> "Do you have a rough target for how much you want to raise?"

> "Is there a way to make this fun or competitive for volunteers — teams, prizes, a leaderboard?"

> "How are you planning to follow up after the event?"
