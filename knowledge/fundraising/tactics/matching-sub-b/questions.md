# Matching Gift Campaign Sub-variant B — Match Not Yet Secured

This sub-variant focuses first on securing the match. Once secured, execution follows Sub-variant A.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have a specific person or organisation in mind who might commit to a match?"

> "How well do you know this person or organisation — is it a warm relationship? A cold ask for a match commitment is very hard to land."

> "Do you have a compelling story or case for why this cause deserves a match commitment? The match donor ask is itself a fundraising conversation — it needs a strong narrative."

> "Do you have — or can we help you prepare — talking points for the match donor conversation?"

## Optimization Questions

> "What amount or range are you hoping the match donor will commit to?"

> "What deadline or campaign window makes sense to propose to the match donor?"

> "How are you planning to approach them — in person, by phone, by email?"
