# Matching Gift Campaign Sub-variant B — Match Not Yet Secured — Execution Plan

This sub-variant focuses on securing the match commitment first. Once secured, execution follows Sub-variant A.

---

## Phase 1 — Secure the Match

**Step 1: Prepare your match donor ask**

The match donor ask is itself a fundraising conversation — it needs a compelling story and a clear, specific proposal.

[Generate match donor talking points. Personalised to: confirmed story, proposed match amount and conditions, campaign timeline, and what public recognition the match donor would receive if they agree. Structure: why this cause matters → why a match works → the specific proposal → what you're asking them to commit to → timeline and next steps.]

**Step 2: Have the conversation**

Approach the potential match donor through your warmest channel — in person if possible, phone if not. Send the talking points in advance if helpful, but be prepared to make the ask conversationally.

**Step 3: Confirm the commitment in writing**

Once the match donor says yes, confirm all terms in writing before doing anything else — total amount, deadline, conditions, and whether their name can be used publicly and in results communications. Do not promote until this is confirmed.

**Step 4: Once confirmed — transition to Sub-variant A**

Follow the Matching Gift Campaign Sub-variant A execution plan from Step 2 onwards (decide whether to name the match donor publicly).
