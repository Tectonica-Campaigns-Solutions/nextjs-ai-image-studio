# In-Person Ask at Community Meetings or Events — Questions

This tactic requires existing in-person touchpoints. Without them it cannot work — redirect to a digital tactic.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you regularly attend or run in-person meetings or events where you could make a fundraising ask?"

> "Do you have a story you can share in person that connects people to why you're fundraising?"

> "Do you have — or can we help you prepare — a short script or talking points for the ask?"

> "Do you have something to leave behind after the ask — a card with a QR code, a flyer, or similar?"

> "Do you know roughly how much you want to ask people for?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations? If so, does your org have a process for handling and tracking cash?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have contact details for the people you'll be asking — email or phone for follow-up?"

> "Have any of these people donated to your org or group before?"

> "How are you planning to follow up after the event — a thank you, an update on impact?"
