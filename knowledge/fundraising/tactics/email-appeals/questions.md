# Email Fundraising Appeals — Questions

## Entry Question

> "Are you planning to send this from your own personal email account, or using your group's contact list and an email platform?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have a contact list with email addresses for the people you want to reach?"

> "Are you aware of the email marketing rules that apply in your region — for example CAN-SPAM in the US, GDPR in Europe, or CASL in Canada? We'll need to make sure your campaign is compliant before sending."

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have a story or personal connection to the cause that we can build the email around?"

> "How engaged is your list — have these people heard from you or the org recently, or is this more of a cold send?"

> "Is there a specific donation amount you want to suggest?"

> "Are you planning a single email or a series? A short series typically converts better than a single ask."

> "Who will the email come from — your name, the group name, or the org name? We'll set up the email to come from the right sender."

> "Are you planning to amplify the email campaign on social media as well?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors after they give?"
