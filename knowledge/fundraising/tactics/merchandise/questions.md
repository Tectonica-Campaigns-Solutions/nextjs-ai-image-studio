# Merchandise / Product Sales — Questions (Tier 2)

## Entry Questions

> "What are you planning to sell — org-branded merchandise, a publication like a cookbook or calendar, or other products like donated goods or handmade items?"

> "Do you already have a design or product ready, or do you need to create one?"

If design needed: "The Tectonica AI image creation helper can help you build merchandise graphics. Let's get that sorted before we move to sales planning."

If publication content needed: "For publication content like copy or editorial text, I can help you draft that directly. Let's work through the content you need before moving to production planning."

> "Is this a time-limited campaign with a specific window, or are you planning to keep this available as an ongoing offering?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Is your product confirmed — do you have inventory, is it in production, or is the design actively being created?"

> "For branded merchandise: does your org have a print-on-demand platform or physical inventory set up?"

> "Have you thought through pricing? We want to make sure the margin actually makes this worth the effort. What are your production costs, and what are you planning to charge?"

> "Who is handling fulfilment — shipping, delivery, or in-person distribution? This needs to be confirmed before you launch."

> "Are you planning to sell in person, online, or both?"

> "For publications: how much time do you have? Producing a cookbook, calendar, or zine takes weeks or months — if the timeline is tight, we may need to look at a faster option."

> "Do you have a way to capture buyer email addresses so we can confirm orders and follow up?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have contact details for people you want to promote this to — email, phone, or messaging apps?"

> "Are you active on social media? We can help you create promotional content including product visuals."

> "If selling with a group, how many members can help promote or sell?"

> "Is there a story or cause connection we can feature alongside the product to make the ask more compelling?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with buyers — a thank you, an update on how the funds are being used?"
