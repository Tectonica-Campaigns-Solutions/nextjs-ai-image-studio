# Tribute / Memorial Giving Sub-variant A — Memorial

The most emotionally sensitive tactic in the system. Tone throughout must be warmer and slower than any other tactic.

## Entry Question

> "Is this tied to a recent passing, an upcoming anniversary or remembrance date, or a standalone tribute with no fixed timeline?"

- Recent passing → simplified, faster plan; family awareness gate is critical
- Anniversary / remembrance → standard timeline
- Standalone → most flexibility, full plan

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Can you tell me about the person being honoured? Their story is the heart of this campaign — we want to make sure it's told with care and dignity."

> "Are the family or next of kin aware of and comfortable with this fundraiser? This is especially important if the passing was recent."

> "Do you have a personal network connected to this person — family, friends, colleagues, community members — who we can reach out to?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have photos of the person being honoured that we can use on the page?"

> "Do you have contact details for the people you want to reach — email, phone, or messaging apps?"

> "Are you active on social media? We can help you share the tribute page sensitively and appropriately."

> "Is there a specific donation amount you want to suggest?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors — an update, a thank you, a note about how the funds will be used?"
