# House Parties / Salon Fundraisers — Questions

Requires a confirmed host with a warm personal network. No host = hard stop.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have someone willing to host — to open their home or a space and personally invite their network?"

> "Does the host have a warm personal network they can invite — people they know well enough to ask face to face?"

> "Do you have a story to share at the event that connects guests emotionally to the cause?"

> "Do you have — or can we help you prepare — talking points for the ask during the event?"

> "Do you have something to leave with guests — a card with a QR code, a flyer, or a pledge form?"

> "Do you know roughly how much you want to ask guests for?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations at the event? If so, does your org have a process for handling cash?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

**Note for solo path:** If the leader is doing this alone, they are both the host and the person making the ask. Acknowledge this directly: "You'll be carrying both roles here — host and asker. That's absolutely doable and we'll plan for it."

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have contact details for guests ahead of the event — email or phone to send invitations and reminders?"

> "For each person helping — what is their role? For example: host, co-host, greeter, ask lead, follow-up coordinator. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "How many people can help with planning and logistics?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with guests after the event?"
