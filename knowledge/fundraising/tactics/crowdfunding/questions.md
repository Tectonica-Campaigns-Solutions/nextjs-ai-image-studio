# Crowdfunding — Questions

Uses a single shared campaign page. If individual pages per participant are wanted, redirect to peer-to-peer fundraising.

## Entry Questions

> "Are you planning one shared campaign page that everyone promotes, or would you like each group member to have their own individual fundraising page?"

- One shared page → proceed with crowdfunding
- Individual pages per participant → redirect to peer-to-peer

> "Is this a time-limited campaign with a specific goal and deadline, or are you planning to keep the page live as an ongoing fundraising presence?"

- Time-limited → standard campaign structure
- Evergreen → ongoing page with periodic promotion

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have a story — personal, from your group, or from someone your org has helped — that will compel people to donate?"

> "Do you have photos or video you can use on the crowdfunding page? A page with no visuals significantly underperforms."

> "Do you have a social media presence or a personal network large enough to seed the campaign with early donations?"

> "Can you identify at least 5 to 10 people who would donate in the first 48 to 72 hours after launch? Early momentum is critical — a campaign that launches cold rarely takes off."

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have contact details for your network — email, phone, or messaging apps like WhatsApp?"

> "Are you active on social media? Which platforms, and how engaged is your audience?"

> "For each person helping — what is their role? For example: campaign lead, early donor recruiter, social media amplifier, update writer, follow-up coordinator. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "For each active solicitor — what is their primary network? For example: workplace, faith community, neighbourhood, family. This helps calibrate ask amounts and messaging tone per segment."

> "How many group members can help promote and share the campaign?"

> "Do you have a plan for following up with donors — campaign updates, a thank you, a final push?"
