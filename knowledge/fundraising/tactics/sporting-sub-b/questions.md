# Sporting Events Sub-variant B — Self-Organised Event or Tournament

Group organises their own sporting event or tournament. Higher overhead than Sub-variant A. Group only.

## Entry Question

> "What kind of sporting event or tournament are you thinking of organising?"

> "For the fundraising side — are you planning each participant to fundraise individually (their own personal page), or a single shared campaign page everyone promotes?"

- Individual pages → fundraising track follows peer-to-peer execution
- Single shared page → fundraising track follows crowdfunding execution

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have enough volunteers to organise and run the event?"

> "For each person involved — what role are they taking on? For example: event coordinator, registration lead, cash handler, promotions, logistics, social media. We'll document this in your plan."

> "Do you have a venue confirmed or a strong lead on one?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations at the event? If so, does your org have a process for handling and tracking cash?"

> "Have you checked whether you need any permits or permissions for this event?"

> "Do you have materials for the event — signage, QR codes, donation instructions?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have a story or cause connection to feature at the event?"

> "Is there a competitive element — teams, prizes, a leaderboard — that could drive both participation and fundraising?"

> "Are you active on social media? We can help you promote the event."

> "How are you planning to follow up after the event?"
