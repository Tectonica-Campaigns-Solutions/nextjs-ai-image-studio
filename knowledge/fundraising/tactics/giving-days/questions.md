# Online Giving Days — Questions (Tier 2)

Requires the org to be participating. If not, redirect to Tribute Sub-variant B (occasion/seasonal giving).

## Entry Question

> "Is your org registered and running a campaign for this giving day?"

If no: "It looks like your org isn't running a giving day campaign right now. But you can still fundraise around this moment — let's look at running your own occasion or seasonal giving campaign instead."

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Which giving day are we working with, and what is the exact date? Let's confirm this before anything else."

> "How much time do we have before the giving day? That will shape how ambitious we can be with the plan."

> "Has your org confirmed the campaign assets are ready — donation page link, graphics, and messaging? We'll need those before you can start promoting."

> "Do you have a personal network or group you can activate to amplify the campaign?"

> "Are you active on social media? Social amplification is the primary driver for giving day campaigns."

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Does your org have a matching gift running alongside the giving day? If so, that's your most powerful message — let's make sure it leads everything you share."

> "Do you have a personal story or connection to the cause that you can share alongside the org's materials?"

> "Do you have contact details for your audience — email, phone, or messaging apps — to supplement your social outreach?"

> "If fundraising with a group — for each person helping, what is their role? For example: social media lead, personal outreach lead, email amplifier, follow-up coordinator. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "How many group members can help share and amplify?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors after the giving day?"
