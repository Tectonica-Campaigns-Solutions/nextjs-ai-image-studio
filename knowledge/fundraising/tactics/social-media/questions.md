# Social Media Fundraisers — Questions

A single post rarely converts. This tactic requires a content series.

## Entry Question

> "What type of social media fundraiser are you thinking of — using a platform's built-in donation tool, running a campaign that drives people to your donation page, or coordinating your group to share and amplify across their own channels?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Which social media platforms do you and your audience actually use? Let's make sure we're focusing on the right ones."

> "Do you have photos, graphics, or video to use? Social media fundraisers without visual content perform significantly worse."

> "Do you have a story or personal connection to the cause that we can build the content around?"

> "Are you planning more than one post? A single post rarely converts — we'll need at least a short series of content across the campaign window."

**If using platform-native donation tool:**
> "Is your org registered as a nonprofit on this platform? If not, we'll use a campaign-to-donation-page approach instead."

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "For each person helping — what is their role? For example: content creator, post scheduler, community manager, donor follow-up lead. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "How many group members can help share and amplify content across their own channels?"

> "Is there a specific donation amount you want to suggest in your posts?"

> "Do you have a follow-up plan — thanking donors publicly, sharing progress updates, a final push post?"
