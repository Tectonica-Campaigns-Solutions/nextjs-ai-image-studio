# Local Business Partnerships — Questions

Requires a confirmed business with a warm relationship. Nothing can be planned until the partnership is in writing.

## Entry Question

> "What type of local business partnership are you thinking of — a percentage-of-sales night, a direct sponsorship or donation, or a co-promotion arrangement?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have a specific business in mind, and do you have a warm relationship with someone there — an owner, manager, or contact who knows you or your group?"

> "Is the business confirmed and have they committed to the partnership?"

> "Do you have a concrete plan for how your group will promote this — how you'll drive traffic or audience to the business or event?"

> "Has the business agreed to promote this on their end as well — through their social media, in-store signage, or to their customer base?"

> "Are the terms of the partnership confirmed in writing — including the donation amount or percentage, the date or window, and what both sides will do to promote it?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Is the business donating cash, a percentage of sales, or goods and services? If goods or services, we'll need to think about how to value and acknowledge that."

> "Do you have contact details for your audience — email, phone, or messaging apps — to promote the partnership?"

> "Are you active on social media? We can help you create promotional content for the event."

> "How are you planning to follow up after the partnership — a thank you to the business, an update to your supporters?"
