# Walk / Run / Ride-a-Thon (Self-Organised) — Questions

Group only. Solo leaders should be redirected to Sporting Events Sub-variant A. Participants must be willing to actively fundraise — not just walk.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Are your group members willing to actively fundraise — to ask people in their networks to sponsor them — not just show up and participate?"

> "For each person actively involved — what role are they taking on? For example: campaign coordinator, individual fundraiser, logistics lead, social media promoter, event day volunteer. We'll document this in your plan."

> "Do you have a plan to help each participant seed their personal fundraising page with a few donations before they share it publicly? Pages that launch cold rarely take off."

> "Do you have a story or cause connection we can give participants to use in their personal fundraising asks?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations at the event? If so, does your org have a process for handling and tracking cash?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "How many people in your group are participating and actively fundraising?"

> "Do you have a team target alongside individual ones — a group fundraising goal?"

> "Are there any incentives for top fundraisers — recognition, prizes, a leaderboard?"

> "Do participants have social media presence they can use to promote their pages?"

> "Do you need route permits or any other logistics sorted for the event day?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors after the event?"
