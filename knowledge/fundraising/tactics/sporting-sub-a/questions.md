# Sporting Events Sub-variant A — External Event Participation

Participating in an external event (marathon, obstacle course, cycling race, sponsored challenge) as charity fundraisers. Lower overhead than Sub-variant B. Works solo or with a group.

## Entry Question

> "Is your entry in the event confirmed, or are you still planning to register?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Is your entry in the event confirmed?"

> "Does this event have a minimum fundraising requirement tied to your entry? If so, let's factor that into your goal-setting."

> "Do you have a personal story or connection to the cause that you can share in your fundraising asks? For challenge-based fundraising, the personal narrative is everything."

> "Can you identify at least 5 to 10 people who would donate early to seed your page before you share it broadly?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have contact details for your network — email, phone, or messaging apps?"

> "Are you active on social media? Training updates and behind-the-scenes content can significantly boost donations for challenge-based fundraising."

> "If doing this with a group — for each person participating, what is their role? For example: individual fundraiser, team captain, social media lead, logistics coordinator. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "If doing this with a group, how many people are participating and fundraising?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors — updates during training, a post-event thank you?"
