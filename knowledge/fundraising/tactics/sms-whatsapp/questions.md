# SMS / Text / WhatsApp Fundraising — Questions

Opt-in is a legal requirement. No opt-in = hard stop.

## Entry Question

> "How do you primarily communicate with your contacts via mobile — SMS/text, WhatsApp, another messaging app, or a mix?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have phone numbers or messaging app contacts for the people you want to reach?"

> "Have the people on your list opted in to receive messages from you or your group? This is a legal requirement in most places — sending without consent can damage your relationships and your org's reputation."

> "How are you planning to send — personally messaging people one by one, a broadcast list, or through a platform?"

**WhatsApp broadcast note:** For a broadcast message to be delivered, recipients must have your number saved in their contacts. If you're not sure they do, a personal message or group chat may work better.

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have a personal story or connection to the cause that you can share in your message?"

> "Is there a specific donation amount you want to suggest?"

> "Do you have images or visuals you could include to make the message more compelling?"

> "How are you planning to follow up after the initial message?"
