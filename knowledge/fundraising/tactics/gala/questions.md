# Gala / Dinner Fundraiser — Questions

Group only. Most complex tactic on the list. Requires venue, committee, ticketing, and a structured in-room ask.

## Entry Question

> "What scale of event are you thinking — a small community dinner for around 20 to 50 people, a mid-size fundraising dinner for 50 to 150, or a larger formal gala for 150 or more?"

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "How much time do you have before the event? Let's make sure the timeline is realistic for the scale you have in mind before we go further."

> "Do you have a venue confirmed or a strong lead on one? Nothing else can be planned until this is locked."

> "Do you have a committee or working group helping you plan this? A gala is not a one-person job."

> "For each committee member — what role are they taking on? For example: event coordinator, ticketing lead, sponsorship lead, programme and entertainment, cash and donations lead, guest relations, social media and promotion. We'll document this in your plan."

> "Do you have a ticketing mechanism in place — a platform for selling tickets online or managing registrations?"

> "Are you planning to accept cash donations on the night? If so, does your org have a process for handling cash?"

> "Have you planned a fundraising moment during the event itself — a live appeal, a pledge moment, a silent auction? A gala without a structured ask is a missed opportunity."

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

**For mid-size and large events:**

> "Is catering confirmed or in progress?"

> "Do you have org staff or support available to help manage the event?"

> "Have you checked what permits or licenses you need — including a liquor license if you're serving alcohol?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Have you identified any local businesses or contacts who might sponsor the event?"

> "Is there a compelling story or cause moment you can build into the programme?"

> "Do you have entertainment or a programme planned?"

> "Do you have contact details for your guest list — email for invitations and follow-up?"

> "Are you planning a silent auction alongside the main ask?"

> "Do you have a specific ticket price and donation ask amount in mind? We can help you think through the numbers."

> "How are you planning to follow up with guests after the event?"
