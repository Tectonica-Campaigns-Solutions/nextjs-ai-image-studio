# Tribute / Memorial Giving Sub-variant B — Occasion / Seasonal

Fundraising tied to a holiday, cultural occasion, or awareness moment. Extended occasions (Ramadan, Christmas, Diwali) span weeks and require a different content model.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "What occasion or cultural moment are you fundraising around, and when exactly does it fall? Let's confirm the date before we do anything else."

> "How much time do we have before the occasion? If it's less than a week away, we'll need to simplify — let's be realistic about what's achievable."

> "Has your org provided messaging, assets, or a campaign frame for this occasion? The group activates around org-provided framing — let's confirm those materials exist before we build your plan."

> "Does this occasion have personal or community significance for you and the people you're reaching out to?"

> "Do you have a way to capture donor email addresses at the point of giving?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Do you have a story or community narrative connected to this occasion that we can build the campaign around?"

> "Do you have contact details for your audience — email, phone, or messaging apps?"

> "Are you active on social media? Occasion-based giving tends to travel well on social, especially for culturally significant moments."

> "Is there a specific donation amount you want to suggest?"

> "How are you planning to follow up with donors after the occasion?"
