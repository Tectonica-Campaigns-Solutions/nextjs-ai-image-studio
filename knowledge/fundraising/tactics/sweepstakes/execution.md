# Raffles Sub-variant B — Sweepstakes — Execution Plan

Sweepstakes follow the same structure as raffles with a lighter legal burden — free entry, donation optional.

## Phase 1 — Legal and Logistics

**Step 1: Confirm legal compliance**

Lighter than raffles but still jurisdiction-dependent. Confirm with your org whether there are any local rules that apply to sweepstakes in your area before proceeding.

**Step 2: Confirm your prize**

Confirm: what the prize is, its approximate value, and how it will be delivered to the winner.

**Step 3: Confirm your entry mechanism**

How are people entering — through your donation page, a form, social media? Confirm the mechanism and test it before promoting. A standard donation page may suffice if it supports entry capture.

**Step 4: Set your suggested donation amount**

Entry is free — but a suggested donation alongside free entry can significantly boost giving.

Confirm what needs approval before going out.
