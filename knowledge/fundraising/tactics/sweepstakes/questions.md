# Raffles Sub-variant B — Sweepstakes

Free entry, donation optional. Lighter legal requirements than a raffle but still jurisdiction-dependent.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Have you checked the sweepstakes rules in your area? The rules are generally lighter than for raffles since no purchase is required to enter, but they still vary by jurisdiction. Has your org given you guidance on this?"

> "Do you have a prize confirmed?"

> "How are people going to enter — through your donation page, a form, social media?"

> "Do you have a way to capture email addresses from entrants so we can notify the winner and follow up?"

## Optimization Questions

> "Is there a suggested donation amount you want to encourage alongside free entry?"

> "Are you active on social media? Sweepstakes tend to travel well on social — a sharing incentive can significantly boost reach. Note: in some jurisdictions, requiring sharing as a condition of entry is restricted — check before adding this."

> "Do you have contact details for people you want to promote this to?"

> "How are you planning to draw the winner and announce the result?"
