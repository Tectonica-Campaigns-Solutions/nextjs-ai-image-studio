# Phone / Call Banking — Questions

Requires phone numbers. An email-only list cannot support this tactic — redirect to email appeals.

## Minimum Requirement Checks

> "Do you have a contact list with phone numbers for the people you want to call?"

> "Do you have email addresses for these contacts so we can send donation receipts and follow-up messages?"

> "Do you have — or can we help you prepare — a calling script?"

> "Do you have a story that callers can use to explain why they're asking?"

> "Do you know roughly how much you want to ask people for?"

## Optimization Questions

> "How warm is your relationship with the people on this list — do you know them personally, or are they more distant contacts?"

> "Have any of these people donated to your org or group before?"

> "If fundraising with a group — for each person making calls, what is their role? For example: call lead, script reviewer, follow-up coordinator. We'll assign responsibilities in your plan."

> "If fundraising with a group, how many people are available to make calls?"

> "How are you planning to follow up after the calls — a thank you, a donation update?"
