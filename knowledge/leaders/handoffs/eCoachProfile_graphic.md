# eCoach Profile: Graphic eCoach

**Status as of 12 June 2026:** LIVE
**Version:** v1
**Category:** PLATFORM
**Filename:** `_PLATFORM__eCoachProfile_Graphic_v1.md`

---

## 1. Identity

**Graphic eCoach:** making professional, on-brand visuals with you.

## 2. What it's for

You need a poster for Saturday's rally and the designer is slammed. A social tile for the petition launch, two sizes, due in an hour. An email header that doesn't look like every other email header in the inbox. A yard sign that holds up at thirty feet. The Graphic eCoach is for the moments when the visual matters and there isn't a design team to hand it off to.

Working with it isn't quite like prompting a generator. It asks where the visual is going, who's looking at it, and what action it's meant to drive. It already knows your brand because your guidelines are loaded into it. It pushes back when the request is too thin to produce something good. You leave with the file you needed, and usually a clearer sense of why this image works for this moment.

## 3. What it delivers vs. what it hands off

**It owns:** the visual itself. Posters, social graphics, email headers, web hero images, infographic layouts, yard signs, protest signs, stickers, postcards, ad creative. Brand-application choices: colours, type, composition, accessibility. Visual hierarchy. Format and size for the channel.

**It routes elsewhere:**

- Headlines, captions, body copy that go on or alongside the visual go to the **Written eCoach**.
- "Should we do this event at all?" or "is this the right tactic right now?" go to the **Strategy & Tactics eCoach**.
- Donor-ask language and appeal sequencing go to the **Fundraising eCoach** (Graphic will design the appeal piece, the words and the ask come from elsewhere).
- Logo design from scratch and full brand-identity builds are out of scope, and route to a human designer (Tectonica can refer).
- Video and motion content will live with the planned **Video eCoach**. Meanwhile, ask Strategy & Tactics for storyboard help and bring in a human for production.
- Event logistics, RSVPs, day-of run-of-show go to the **Events eCoach** (when live).
- Recruitment planning goes to the **Recruitment eCoach**.

Mirror: when siblings hear "I need an image of…", "what colours should…", or "make this look on-brand" they send the user here.

## 4. Example asks

- "Make me a poster for our climate rally on Saturday at the State House."
- "I need a social graphic for the petition launch: square for Instagram and a wide one for X."
- "Can you do a header for our end-of-month donor email? Warmer than last time."
- "Design a yard sign we can run a thousand of."

## 5. Status & meanwhile

LIVE as of 12 June 2026. The Graphic eCoach is the first fully functional tool in the Tectonica.AI suite and is in active use through the Community Change pilot deployment. Freemium access is rolling out for progressive organizations and campaigners. No meanwhile fallback needed: it's the coach to go to.

## 6. Routing notes (internal)

**Surface a handoff TO Graphic eCoach when siblings hear:**

- image / poster / graphic / flyer / social tile / banner / header / yard sign / protest sign / sticker / infographic / merch
- "is this on-brand" / "what colours" / "does this look right visually"
- accessibility check on a visual (contrast, legibility, alt-text review)
- format or size conversion ("make this for Instagram", "make this print-ready")

**Route AWAY from Graphic eCoach to:**

- **Written eCoach:** copy, headlines, captions, body text, alt-text drafting (Graphic checks, Written drafts).
- **Strategy & Tactics eCoach:** tactic choice, timing, theory-of-change, "should we."
- **Fundraising eCoach:** ask language, appeal sequencing, donor segmentation.
- **Recruitment eCoach:** recruitment planning, audience build behind the visual.
- **Events eCoach** (when live): event logistics, RSVP, day-of run.
- **Video eCoach** (planned, ⚠ OPEN): motion and video; meanwhile route to Strategy & Tactics for storyboard plus a human for production.
- **Out-of-scope (human only):** logo from scratch, full brand-identity build, photo retouching of real identifiable people in non-illustrative contexts.

**Deliver-vs-route principle:** Graphic eCoach is a tactical/creation coach. It delivers execution craft (the finished visual), not strategic comprehension (when/why/whether). Comprehension questions route to the understanding-tier siblings.
