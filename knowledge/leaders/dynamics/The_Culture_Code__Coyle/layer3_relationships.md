# The Culture Code — Relationships, Flags & Coverage

## Layer 3 — Relationship Map

- **Belonging cues ↔ The vulnerability loop** — [A6.5] × [A4.3]: safety (the steady cues that say *we're connected*) is the *precondition* that makes the riskier exchange of vulnerability possible; the book's first two skills are sequential — glue, then muscle. (Entries: "Belonging cues," "The vulnerability loop")
- **The leader goes first ↔ Engineering candor (AARs/BrainTrusts)** — [A1.3] × [A8.2]: the leader's "I screwed that up" is what opens the safe channel that an AAR or BrainTrust then institutionalizes. (Entries: "The leader goes first," "Engineering candor")
- **Cooperation as a muscle ↔ The vulnerability loop** — [A6.3] × [A4.3]: shared-risk activities (Log PT, the Harold) are just the vulnerability loop repeated thousands of times until cohesion becomes reflex; cohesion is the *result* of cooperating, not its prerequisite. (Entries: "Cooperation is a muscle," "The vulnerability loop")
- **Make the leader disappear ↔ Lead for creativity** — [A9.3] × [A7.3]: stepping back is how a leader protects creative autonomy and develops shared leadership; the engineer-not-captain stance and the empty time-out are the same move. (Entries: "Make the leader disappear," "Lead for proficiency vs. creativity")
- **High-purpose environments ↔ Catchphrases as heuristics** — [A2.1] × [A6.1]: catchphrases are the portable, repeatable *delivery mechanism* for the purpose signals a high-purpose environment floods the zone with. (Entries: "High-purpose environments," "Catchphrases as heuristics")
- **Stories create reality ↔ Name and rank priorities** — [A2.2] × [A2.3]: a ranked priority list is the spine of the group's story; both orient behavior by linking present effort to a future ideal. (Entries: "Stories create reality," "Name and rank priorities")
- **The antibelonging environment ↔ Belonging cues** — [A6.4] × [A6.5]: the missileers are belonging cues run in reverse — the same mechanism that builds safety, absent, produces a self-reinforcing stall. (Entries: "The antibelonging environment," "Belonging cues")
- **Onboarding & threshold moments ↔ Safety needs refreshing** — [A9.7] × [A6.5]: arrivals are the highest-yield moment to send a cue, but the WIPRO effect only holds because cues keep coming; the threshold opens a relationship that must then be sustained. (Entries: "Onboarding and threshold moments," "Safety has to be refreshed")
- **Listening is the catalyst ↔ The relationship-maker** — [A4.3] × [A9.6]: intent listening (Givechi's "say more about that") and Popovich's up-close care are two faces of the same trust-building, coaching skill. (Entries: "Listening is the catalyst," "The relationship-maker")
- **Engineering candor ↔ Crisis crystallizes purpose** — [A6.6] × [A1.6]: the reflective ritual (AAR) is the routine version of what a crisis does once — both turn failure into a shared mental model and sharpened identity. (Entries: "Engineering candor," "Crisis crystallizes purpose")
- **Designing for collisions ↔ Make the leader disappear** — [A5.4] × [A9.3]: both are condition-setting — the leader arranges the environment (or their own absence) so cooperation happens without them. (Entries: "Designing the space for belonging," "Make the leader disappear")
- **Lead for proficiency vs. creativity ↔ Catchphrases as heuristics** — [A7.3] × [A2.3]: the two leadership modes call for two *kinds* of catchphrase — vivid/specific for proficiency, plain/universal for creativity. (Entries: "Lead for proficiency vs. creativity," "Catchphrases as heuristics")

## Cross-source links (for the corpus, not tags)

- **Cohesion follows cooperation** — Coyle ("Cooperation is a muscle") restates Fisher's claim that cohesion is a *result* of a strong launch and good work, not a prerequisite (Fisher Layer 0: "Conformity must be countered"; launch before bonding). Coaches can pair them.
- **Psychological safety / Amy Edmondson** appears in *both* books — Fisher uses her nursing-team error study; Coyle uses her for belonging cues *and* the MICS surgical-learning study. Note the shared source to avoid double-counting evidence.
- **Learning together as the engine** — Coyle's AAR/BrainTrust ("Engineering candor") maps directly onto Fisher's Layer-0 "Learning together is the engine."
- **Stories / purpose** — Coyle's "Stories create reality" sits beside Ganz's public narrative [A2.2]; the Grant scholarship study connects to Pink's intrinsic "purpose" driver [A3.1].

## Excluded from this summary

Cut as irrelevant to leading a small volunteer group (logged for review):

- **Organization- and facility-scale design** — Hsieh's Downtown Project economics ($754M, layoffs), Pixar's and Disney's building architecture, Bank of America's corporate coffee-machine rollout — retained only as the *principle* of proximity/collisions for a small-group leader; the org-scale execution is noted as `H:org-level`.
- **Industry economics and footnotes** — NBA passing-vs-shooting salary data, the Baron–Hannan startup "commitment model" IPO statistics, Dunbar's number, slime-mold and starling biology beyond the heuristic point.
- **Pure historical color** — Christmas Truce military history, Curtis LeMay and the missile program's origins, Bell Labs' invention list, the Coyle family/epilogue coaching narrative — used as illustration only, not summarized as concepts.
- **Hiring-process mechanics** — Zappos' "$2,000 to quit," painstaking multi-stage interviews — noted under threshold moments but not developed, as detailed hiring design leans org-level for most volunteer contexts.
